Use content to create the demand activation converts.

Opportunity03
01 · Opportunity

Activation has a pool. Awareness expands it.

  • Activation converts the ~5% of buyers in-market today.

Why 20M27
25 · The number

Why 20M? Awareness needs scale to move the needle.

  • At 1M/month we sit below the recall threshold in priority regions — noise, not signal
  • Awareness cycle — decision-makers need 3+ exposures within ~4 weeks to retain the message
  • At 20M/month across priority regions, we clear effective reach — results become measurable
Small numbers are measurement noise. 20M is the scale that makes awareness readable.
